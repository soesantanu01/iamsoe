Santanu Soe

I am a Prime Minister's Research Fellow (PMRF) and Joint PhD Scholar at IIT Madras and the University of Melbourne, specializing in mathematical optimization for imaging and machine learning.

I have received the Melbourne Research Fellowship for my doctoral studies at the University of Melbourne.

My research focuses on developing novel algorithms for solving structured optimization problems, with particular emphasis on operator splitting methods, primal-dual algorithms, and structured regularization techniques.

With a strong foundation in functional analysis and convex optimization, I create efficient computational methods that bridge theoretical mathematics with practical applications in medical imaging and large-scale data analysis.
